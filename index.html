About Me

I am a web developer living in Boston.

I have always loved tech and knew it was the field I wanted to have a career in. As a finance major looking for jobs at tech companies, I always found myself scrolling past dozens of engineering and development roles to find that lonely sales representative position I qualified for. It came to the point where I decided I would scroll no more, so I enrolled a twelve-week web Development course to chase my goal.

I completed the course in November, 2016, and joined a new company as a developer in early 2017.

Front End Design

I strive to create intuitive, attractive and highly functional web apps. I am always going back to my projects to optimize performance, test responsiveness, and improve features until the app delivers the best possible experience for the user.

Back End Architecture

I have built RESTful APIs using Ruby on Rails, Flask, and Node.js with Express and Mongoose. The back end is the Bill Belichick to the front end's Tom Brady; it does its job out of the spotlight, but it is the reason the greatest websites are so great.

Golf Handicap Calculator +

This is the first app I ever built and is a simple tic tac toe game with a great Stranger Things theme. It's logic is written in JavaScript and is styled and animated with jQuery and custom Sass. Nelson Cash designed the title font and background.

Sweaters for Turtles

A team built, full stack E-commerce web app for funny sweaters knit for turtles. The front end is built with JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars, BootStrap and Sass. The back end was built with Node.js, Express.js, Mongoose and uses MongoDB.

A website for a non-profit organization that fights to prevent sex trafficking in the Golden Triangle. Originally developed with custom HTML and CSS, it has since been redesigned on Wix to allow editing by others in the organization.
