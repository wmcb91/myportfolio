About Me

I am a web developer living in Boston.

I have always loved tech and knew it was the field I wanted to have a career in. As a finance major trying to get my foot in the door, I always found myself scrolling past dozens of engineering and development roles to find that lonely sales representative position I qualified for. I finally came to a point where I decided I would scroll no more, and made the best decision of my life by enrolling in a Web Development Immersive Program at General Assembly to chase my goal.

I completed the twelve-week course in November, 2016, and am excited to join a team of developers to learn from the best. I am a passionate full stack web developer and am eager to improve my abilities every day.

Skills I will bring you

Full Stack Development

I have experience developing projects from user stories, wireframes and ERDs to fully functional deployed apps. Whether you just want to play Stranger Things themed tic-tac-toe or calculate your golf handicap, I can build a solution for you.

Front End Design

I strive to create intuitive, attractive and highly functional web apps. I am always going back to my projects to optimize responsiveness, improve features and test performance until the app delivers the best possible experience for the user.

Back End Builds

I have built RESTful APIs using Ruby on Rails and Node.js with Express and Mongoose. In my mind, the back end is the Bill Belichick to the front end's Tom Brady; it does its job out of the spotlight, but it is the reason the greatest websites are so great.

Recent Projects

Click the thumbnails for more infomation

Golf Handicap Calculator +

This app keeps a history of a user's rounds of golf & automatically calculates and updates their handicap index as they add new rounds. It uses JavaScript, jQuery, Sass on the front end and the API was built with Ruby on Rails for a PostgreSQL database.

Stranger Tic Tac Toe

This is the first app I ever built and is a simple tic tac toe game with a great Stranger Things theme. It's logic is written in JavaScript and is styled and animated with jQuery and custom Sass. Nelson Cash designed the title font and background.

Sweaters for Turtles

A team built, full stack E-commerce web app for funny sweaters knit for turtles. The front end is built with JavaScript, jQuery, Handlebars, BootStrap and Sass. The back end was built with Node.js, Express.js, Mongoose and uses MongoDB.

Friendsofthaidaughters.org

A website for a non-profit organization that fights to prevent sex trafficking in the Golden Triangle. Originally developed with custom HTML and CSS, it has since been redesigned on Wix to allow editing by others in the organization.
